JOJOFriend is a free, open-source browser extension that automatically finds and applies the best coupon codes at checkout, helping you earn cashback at competitive rates.

Earn Cash Back in One Click
Shop at your favorite online stores—JOJOFriend will notify you when cashback is available. Activate your rewards with a single click, then continue shopping as usual. Your cashback will be tracked automatically and credited to your account.

Why Choose JOJOFriend?
* Security & Transparency: JOJOFriend is open-source and continuously reviewed by a global community of users, ensuring accountability and trust.
* Automatic Savings: Instantly detects and applies the best available coupon codes at checkout.
* Smart Shopping Insights: View Amazon product price history to make more informed purchase decisions.
* Intelligent Coupon Alerts: Get notified when coupons are available while browsing products.
* Exclusive Deals: Access a curated selection of high-value discounts across supported stores.
